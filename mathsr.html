MATHEMATICS

Mathematics includes the study of such topics as Quantity, Structure, Space,and Change.



The process of calculating the total of two or more numbers or amounts is called ADDITION


SUBTRACTION is an arithmetic operation that represents the operation of removing objects from a collection.


The MULTIPLICATION of whole numbers may be thought as a repeated addition.



The DIVISION of two natural numbers is the process of calculating the number of times one number is contained within another one.
